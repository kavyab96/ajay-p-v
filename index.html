Hi, I'm Ajay.

I am a passionate researcher interested in Polymer Science and Material Science, driven by the vision of creating sustainable materials for real-world applications. My work bridges materials science and environmental engineering, with a strong focus on innovation and interdisciplinary collaboration.

My Mission

My mission is to advance sustainable innovation through cutting-edge research in polymer science, additive manufacturing, and wastewater treatment. I aim to develop environmentally responsible materials and technologies that address real-world challenges in microelectronics, biomedical devices, and environmental engineering. Through interdisciplinary collaboration and a commitment to scientific excellence, I strive to contribute to a greener, smarter, and more sustainable future.

Current Research

Fungus-like Additive Materials (FLAM): Exploring bio-inspired, sustainable materials that mimic the structure and growth patterns of fungi for scalable manufacturing.

Large-Scale Manufacturing with Biological Molecules: Engineering materials and processes that leverage biologics for industrial-scale fabrication.

Composites for 3D Printing: Developing high-performance, bio-based composites tailored for next-gen additive manufacturing technologies.

Metal-Polymer Hybrid Systems: Creating multifunctional materials that synergize the strength and conductivity of metals with the flexibility and processability of polymers.

2D & 3D Microelectronics: Innovating in the realm of flexible and miniaturized electronic components using advanced polymeric and composite systems.
